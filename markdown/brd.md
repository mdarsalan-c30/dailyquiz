# 📄 1. BRD (Business Requirements Document)

🎯 **Business Objective**
- Build a mass-friendly daily habit app
- Achieve high retention (daily opens)
- Generate revenue via:
  - AdMob (base)
  - Affiliate (primary)

👥 **Target Audience**
- Students + job seekers
- General users (no niche restriction)
- Age: 16–35

💰 **Revenue Streams**
1. **Ads (AdMob)**
   - After quiz completion
   - Tools screen
2. **Affiliate (MAIN MONEY)**
   - Loans
   - Credit cards
   - Insurance
3. **Sponsored Content (Phase 2)**
   - Paid quizzes

📊 **KPIs**
- DAU / MAU > 40%
- Quiz completion rate > 80%
- D7 retention > 25%
- Revenue / user
